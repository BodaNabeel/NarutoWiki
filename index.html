summary

Many years ago, in the hidden village of Konoha, appeared a great demon fox. When it swung one of it's nine tails, a tsunami occurred. The fourth hokage sealed this demon fox inside a boy in exchange for his own life. Naruto was that boy, and he grew up with no family, and the villagers hated him thinking that he himself was the demon fox. Naruto's dream is to become Hokage, and have the villagers acknowledge him. The anime revolves around development of all the characters that are in Naruto. Mostly the main plot of anime is to establish peace between all major and minor nations. Total 4 wars broke out in the anime and finally after that peace of the shinobi world was secured. This anime throws light on “how hatred leads to nothing”, “how easily even the strongest shinobi’s can be easily manipulated”, “how building honest relations with others can only bring peace in the world”, etc
